Consumer Car Care

At Consumer Car Care (C3), we are committed to revolutionizing the automotive industry through innovative solutions and a customer-centric approach.

Established in Mississauga, Ontario, C3 is a leading provider of add-on products and services for new car purchases. We specialize in offering aftermarket enhancements tailored to personalize your recently acquired vehicle, ensuring a seamless your unique style and experiences.

With our headquarters located in Mississauga and a regional office in Lachine, Quebec, we cater to markets across Ontario and Quebec, offering a range of products including rust protection, fabric protection, paint protection, VIN etching, and extended warranties. Our business model involves collaborating with dealerships, where we negotiate, capture sales, and enjoy profit-sharing partnerships. Through this partnership, both parties share profits, ensuring mutual success and growth.

Driven by our vision, we continually strive to enhance the customer experience, delivering high-quality solutions that meet the evolving needs of the automotive industry. Join us on our journey as we redefine car care and set new standards of excellence.
